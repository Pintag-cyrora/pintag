Marketing OS — TikTok Integration Privacy Policy

Effective: July 14, 2026
Scope: this page covers only Marketing OS, Pintag's internal marketing tool, and what it accesses via TikTok's API. It does not describe how Pintag's real estate marketplace handles listing, buyer, or agent data — see pintag.io for those.

What we access

After you explicitly authorize Marketing OS through TikTok's own login and consent screen, we access, via TikTok's official Display API:

  • Account-level statistics: follower count, following count, total likes, video count.
  • Recent video metadata: title, publish date, view count, like count, comment count, share count.

We do not access private messages, your followers/following lists, comment text, or anything beyond what's listed above — TikTok's API does not expose those to Marketing OS, and we do not seek broader access.

What we never do

Marketing OS never posts, publishes, edits, schedules, or deletes anything on TikTok. It is read-only.

How this data is used

Solely to generate an internal daily summary for Pintag's own marketing team — understanding how our own content is performing. It is never used for advertising targeting, never sold, and never shared with any third party.

What's stored

The OAuth access and refresh tokens needed to keep your account connected are stored in Pintag's private, access-controlled database, used only to make further requests to TikTok's API on your behalf. The statistics and video data themselves are fetched fresh each time Marketing OS runs and are not stored or retained afterward.

Your control

You can revoke Marketing OS's access to your TikTok account at any time from TikTok's own app permissions settings, or by contacting us below.

TikTok's own policies

Your use of TikTok itself continues to be governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
