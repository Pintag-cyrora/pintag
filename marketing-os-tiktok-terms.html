What Marketing OS is

Marketing OS is an internal tool built and used by Pintag (a real estate platform based in Vientiane, Laos) to help our own team understand how our TikTok content is performing. It is not a public product — it is used only by Pintag's own staff, currently in testing.

What it does with TikTok

Using TikTok's official Login Kit and Display API, and only after you explicitly authorize it through TikTok's own login screen, Marketing OS reads account statistics and recent video performance for the TikTok account(s) you connect. It does not post, publish, edit, schedule, or delete anything on TikTok, and never will without a separate, explicit change to both this tool and this page.
