# Xaythany

## Character / Vibe

Xaythany is one of the largest and fastest-growing districts on Vientiane's outskirts, encompassing a mix of rural land, new residential subdivisions, and the area around Wattay International Airport and the Vientiane–Vang Vieng expressway corridor. It has an emerging-suburb feel, with land being actively converted from agricultural to residential use.

## Typical Price Band

Among the most affordable of the seven districts on a per-unit basis, though larger land plots and new development along major infrastructure corridors are appreciating quickly. [to be refined by founder]

## Amenities

Proximity to Wattay International Airport, expressway access, and a growing number of new residential developments and small commercial centers.

## Target Buyer Type

First-time buyers and young families seeking affordability, land investors betting on infrastructure-driven appreciation, and buyers prioritizing airport or expressway access.
