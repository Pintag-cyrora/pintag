# Sisattanak

## Character / Vibe

Sisattanak is one of Vientiane's most established and affluent central districts, home to many government offices, embassies, and international organizations. It has a settled, leafy, low-density feel compared to the busier commercial core near the river, with tree-lined streets and a mix of older colonial-era homes and newer high-end villas.

## Typical Price Band

Among the highest in Vientiane given its central location and embassy-district status. Villas and larger houses command a significant premium over outer districts. [to be refined by founder]

## Amenities

Home to international schools, embassies, upscale restaurants, and easy access to the city center and the Mekong riverside. Well served by established infrastructure relative to newer developing districts.

## Target Buyer Type

Diplomats, senior expatriate staff, established local families, and investors seeking premium long-term holdings. Less suited to budget-conscious first-time buyers or renters.
