# Chanthabouly

## Character / Vibe

Chanthabouly is Vientiane's historic city-center district, home to the Presidential Palace, Wat Sisaket, the night market, and much of the riverside promenade. It is the most urban and commercially dense of the seven districts, with a mix of historic architecture, government buildings, and tourist-facing commerce.

## Typical Price Band

High, driven by scarcity of land and central riverside location, though the district skews toward smaller lots and mixed-use or commercial-adjacent properties rather than large villas. [to be refined by founder]

## Amenities

The core of Vientiane's dining, tourism, and riverside recreation scene, with strong walkability and access to government and commercial services.

Investors interested in commercial or mixed-use property, buyers who prioritize walkability and city-center access, and those seeking rental income from tourism-adjacent short-term or medium-term rentals.
