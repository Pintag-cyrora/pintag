# Saysettha

## Character / Vibe

Saysettha is a large, rapidly developing district east of the city center, known for newer residential developments, the That Luang Marsh area, and a growing concentration of modern housing estates. It has a more suburban, developing-city feel than the older central districts, with a mix of established neighborhoods and active new construction.

## Typical Price Band

Mid-range, with pricing varying significantly between older established pockets and new-build residential projects. Generally more affordable than Sisattanak but higher than the outer districts. [to be refined by founder]

## Amenities

Includes shopping centers, some international schools, hospitals, and the That Luang Marsh urban development zone. Good road access to the city center via several main arteries.

## Target Buyer Type

Middle- and upper-middle-income local families, expatriates seeking newer housing stock, and investors interested in the district's ongoing development momentum.
