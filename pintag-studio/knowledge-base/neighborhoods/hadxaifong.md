# Hadxaifong

## Character / Vibe

Hadxaifong lies south of the city center along the Mekong River, with a mix of residential neighborhoods, agricultural land, and riverside communities. It has a quieter, more residential character than the central districts, appealing to buyers who want proximity to the city without its density.

## Typical Price Band

Moderate to affordable, with riverside and main-road-adjacent land carrying a premium over interior plots. [to be refined by founder]

## Amenities

Riverside access, local markets, and a growing number of residential developments, with reasonable but less extensive infrastructure than the central districts.

## Target Buyer Type

Local families seeking a quieter residential setting, buyers interested in riverside land, and value-oriented investors looking at longer-term district growth.
