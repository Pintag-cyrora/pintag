# Sikhottabong

## Character / Vibe

Sikhottabong sits along the Mekong River to the west of the city center, with a mix of established residential neighborhoods and industrial or logistics activity near the river and main roads. It has a more working-city character than the embassy districts, balancing residential streets with commercial and light-industrial zones.

## Typical Price Band

Moderate, generally below the central districts, with some riverside pockets commanding a premium over the district average. [to be refined by founder]

## Amenities

Access to Mekong riverside areas, local markets, and reasonable connectivity to the city center, along with some industrial and logistics infrastructure.

## Target Buyer Type

Value-conscious local buyers and renters, and investors interested in riverside land or properties tied to logistics and light-industrial activity.
