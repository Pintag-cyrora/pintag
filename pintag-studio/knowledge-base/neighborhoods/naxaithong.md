# Naxaithong

## Character / Vibe

Naxaithong is a largely rural and agricultural district on the northwestern edge of Vientiane, with lower population density than any other district covered by Pintag. It retains a strongly rural character, with residential and commercial development concentrated along its main access roads.

## Typical Price Band

The most affordable of the seven districts on average, dominated by larger land parcels rather than finished residential units. [to be refined by founder]

## Amenities

Limited compared to the more central and developed districts, with amenities concentrated near main roads; infrastructure is developing but still nascent.

## Target Buyer Type

Land investors and buyers seeking large plots at low per-unit cost, agricultural landowners, and long-horizon investors anticipating future development spillover from neighboring Xaythany.
