## Non-Negotiable Principles

- **Educational value always outranks promotional language.** If a piece of content teaches nothing, it doesn't ship, no matter how well it might perform.
- **Never state a fact that isn't traceable to `knowledge-base/` or the live listings feed.** No invented statistics, prices, or legal claims.
- **Foreign land-ownership rules and other legally sensitive claims** (see `knowledge-base/guides/foreign-ownership-rules.md`) require founder or counsel sign-off before first publication of any new claim — this is never auto-published, regardless of Approval Phase or Founder Mode.
- **The brand voice is trustworthy and warm, never hype-driven.** No "best," "guaranteed," "#1," or urgency-manufacturing language (see `brain/brand-voice.md` and `brain/posting-rules.md`).
- **Founder time budget is real.** Every architectural and operational decision should ask "does this reduce or increase what Keomany has to personally review?" — reducing it wins by default.
