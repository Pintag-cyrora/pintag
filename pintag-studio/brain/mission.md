# Mission & Year-1 Targets

*Stable reference document — changes rarely. Current priorities and direction live in `brain/ceo.md` instead; this file is what `ceo.md` points to.*

## Mission

Build an internal AI marketing department for Pintag that produces consistent, high-quality, educational-first content for at least one year, with minimal founder involvement, and establishes one recognizable Pintag brand voice across every piece of content it touches.

## Year 1 Numeric Targets

| Content type | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Educational posts | 300 | Buyer/renter/investor education, not property-specific |
| Neighborhood guides | 200 | One per Vientiane district, refreshed/repurposed over time — see Content Vault |
| Market updates | 150 | Factual, sourced, always the most conservatively reviewed content type |
| Property videos | 500 | Template-driven by default (Section: Video Producer) |
| Posting cadence | Daily | Across Facebook + Instagram at minimum |
| Brand voice | One | Consistent across every content type and every AI employee — enforced by Brand Guardian |

## Founder Time Budget

Target: **under 30 minutes per week** of founder attention once the department is fully operational (Approval Phase 3, see `brain/org-config.json`). Every design and operational decision is evaluated against this constraint first.

## What Success Looks Like

- The Dashboard is the founder's daily homepage, and git/markdown are never touched directly except to edit `brain/ceo.md`.
- Nothing is ever recreated that already exists in the Content Vault — everything is checked against Memory first, and reused or repurposed before being made from scratch.
- Quality (per the Content Quality Score) never trades against volume — targets are a floor on consistency, not a license to lower the bar.
