# Posting Rules

*Enforced by Brand Guardian before scheduling, and by Publisher at publish time.*

## Cadence

- Minimum one post per day across Facebook + Instagram combined, per `brain/mission.md`.
- No content type should go more than 10 days without a new or repurposed item, to keep coverage of all four pillars balanced across the year.

## Platform Rules

**Facebook**
- Captions may run longer (2–4 short paragraphs); native video and image posts preferred over external links where possible.
- Always include a plain-language CTA — never a bare link with no context.

**Instagram**
- Caption front-loads the hook in the first line (visible before "more").
- Hashtag block: 5–10 relevant tags (district, property type, Lao real estate, Pintag brand tag), no hashtag stuffing.
- Carousels preferred over single images for educational and neighborhood-guide content.

**TikTok (Platform Phase 2, not yet active)**
- Rules to be defined when this platform is enabled — do not post here until `org-config.json.platforms.tiktok.active` is `true`.

## Banned Claims / Language

- No absolute superlatives ("best," "#1," "guaranteed") — see `brain/brand-voice.md`.
- No legal or tax claims beyond what's verified in `knowledge-base/guides/`. Foreign-ownership content in particular requires founder/counsel sign-off before first publication of any new claim.
- No manufactured scarcity or urgency not grounded in a real, verifiable fact (e.g., don't claim "only one left" unless the listings feed confirms it).
- No comparative claims naming specific competitors.

## CTA Consistency

Every content type has one primary CTA family, checked by Brand Guardian:
- Educational posts → "Learn more" / explore related guide
- Neighborhood guides → "Browse listings in [district]"
- Market updates → "See the full report" / "Talk to an agent"
- Property videos → "View this listing" / "Book a viewing"

## Disclosure

Any AI-assisted or AI-generated visual/video should not misrepresent a specific property's condition — property videos must be built from that listing's actual photos, never generic/stock imagery presented as the property itself.
